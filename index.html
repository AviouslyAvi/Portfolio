Who I Am

The Alchemist

I'm the kind of producer who cares less about being a hitmaker and more about building a space artists can step into — designing the lighting, the gravity, the mood — then letting the artist move around inside it.

At my core, I'm a problem-solver and creator. I love starting from silence, but I also thrive on scraps: weird fragments, half-finished ideas, rough voice notes. I take what exists and turn it into something organic, intentional, and alive.

I'm obsessed with texture — not just clean sounds, but sounds that feel touchable. I might record the ambience of a room, mangle a voice, capture an everyday moment, and sculpt it into a pad, a bass hit, a transition that exists nowhere else. My tracks have a fingerprint that can't be replicated.

Production & Arrangement

Songs with direction and life — journeys that breathe, move, and surprise. I build the groove, the space, and the story arc, then let it unfold naturally.

Sound Design

I transform field recordings, voices, and found sounds into unique textures — atmospheric pads, bass, drums, risers — that exist nowhere else and can't be replicated.

Finishing & Finalization

I help bring projects across the line — practical, efficient, and intentional — without losing the soul that made the song worth making in the first place.

Teaching & Mentorship

Personalized lessons that explain concepts multiple ways until they land. I meet you where you are and help you realize your own creative potential.

Ideal Collaborators

The energies I work best with

The Chaotic Genius

You throw out sounds that don't make sense at first. You suggest things that feel risky. Together, we create something that makes listeners lean in and ask — "wait, what was that?"

The Disciplined Partner

You bring structure and consistency. That framework frees me to experiment — to introduce unconventional sounds that surprise you. The result is tight and alive.

The Emotionally Attuned

You might not have formal training, but you feel what the music is trying to say. You give me a mirror for the emotions I'm building. Together, we hit the right nerve.
